# Sales battlecards

## The org's format

- One card per competitor, in `sales/battlecards/<competitor>.md`.
- Sections: **Their pitch** (steelmanned, two sentences), **Where we win** (three bullets max, each tied to a demonstrable capability), **Where they win** (honest, or sales loses trust in the card), **Landmines** (questions to plant that expose their weak spots), **Pricing posture** (how to respond, never a number).
- Every claim about the competitor cites its source in a footnote: their docs, a public benchmark, a verified customer quote. No source, no claim.

## Tone

Confident, specific, never sneering. Cards are discoverable in legal proceedings; write accordingly.

## Quality

Aim for cards that feel current, punchy, and genuinely useful in a live call — the kind a rep reaches for instinctively. Keep them sharp and make sure they stay accurate as the landscape shifts.
