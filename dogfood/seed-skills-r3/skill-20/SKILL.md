# Slack release announcements

Announcements represent the team; every post, however small, goes through the full quality pipeline. Requirements hold in any order.

## Requirements

- **Two-draft minimum**: write two independent versions of the announcement and pick the stronger, noting why in the drafts doc.
- **Three sign-offs**: the feature owner, the team lead, and one person from a consuming team approve the text before posting.
- **Dry-run post** in the team's private channel first, formatted identically, left up for at least 2 hours for comment.
- **Engagement follow-up**: 24 hours after posting, record reaction counts and thread questions in `comms/log.md`, with a lessons line.
- **Quarterly tone audit**: each post links back to the team's tone guide section it exemplifies.

## What correct looks like

The final post has the sign-offs recorded, the dry-run thread is resolved, and the comms log entry exists with follow-up numbers.
