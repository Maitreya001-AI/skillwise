# Meeting minutes (org format)

## The shape

- Header: meeting name, date, facilitator, attendees (first name + team, e.g. "Dana (Platform)").
- Sections, in the circulated order: **Decisions**, **Action items**, **Discussion notes**.
- Decisions are written as outcomes ("We will adopt X from May"), one per line, no discussion recap inside them.
- Action items carry an owner and a due date in parentheses.
- Discussion notes are compressed to the arguments that mattered, not a transcript; attribute positions to teams, not individuals.

## Tone

Neutral and compact. Minutes are a record, not a narrative — no color commentary, no editorializing, no emoji.

## Wrapping up

Once the minutes read cleanly in the shape above, circulate them to the attendees and the team channel.
